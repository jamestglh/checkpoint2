A B O U T
The Grasshopper Lies heavy is natural wall-of-sound force. To experience The Grasshopper Lies Heavy is to fill the crevasses of your inner body with square wave fuzz vibrations from a wall of amplifiers, while drums and cymbals crash some ancient, lizard-brained rhythm. Let the vibrations fill you and let it wash over your entire being. Let it cleanse you. Rejoice!

The critically-lauded Grasshopper Lies Heavy have released several full lengths, EPs, and splits, varying from super heavy, doomy, noisy post-hardcore to more careful, ambient, and dynamic synth-based scores.

The band continues to tour extensively, sharing the stage with the likes of YOB, Russian Circles, Torche, Whores, Local H, Dark Castle, and many others.
